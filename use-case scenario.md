                
## Mashup Web Application 

## Input field
- postcode 

## School 
- government school
- non-government school   


## Output
- government school   
  - school gender
  - street
  - selective school entry score
  - attendance rate
  - specific information 
    - schooling
    - suburb
    - postcode
    - code
- non-government school   
  - school gender
  - street
  - specific information
    - schooling
    - suburb
    - postcode
    - code
